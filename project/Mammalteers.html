3 Mammalteers Movie Project

This project really allowed me to put my CSS and HTML skills to work as me and my partners needed to figure out a way to make a poster for a movie that is easy to read and pretty, this project really allowed me to use these skills but in a creative, not boring way.

Me and my partners Kiara and Dianis decided to make a comedy action movie about 3 animals who all come together to use their specialized skills to rob a bank, we came up with this idea by deciding our favorite animals, Kiara chose a rat (not her favorite just a random one) Dianis chose a cat, and I chose a raccoon because they're cool. The project itself was actually pretty easy, you know just come up with an idea and execute it using html and css, something that we already know pretty well. But we did struggle on coming up with ideas and planning, since we had 3 partners instead of 2 it was pretty difficult to plan out who did what in our group, though we eventually came together and decided roles.
